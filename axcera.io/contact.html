Questions? We have Answers

For additional questions, contact our team at hello@nexus.io
How can I contact Nexus for a demo or partnership?

You can contact Nexus by filling out the form on this page or by emailing hello@nexus.io.
Once submitted, a representative will respond within one business day to schedule a personalized product demonstration or discuss partnership opportunities. Firms can also request access to platform documentation and integration details during the call.

Where are Nexus’s offices located?

Nexus operates globally with offices in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, and Limassol, Cyprus. The company also supports clients through remote teams based across Europe, Asia, and North America. This international presence allows Nexus to provide localized support and maintain coverage across all time zones.

How fast does Nexus respond to contact requests?

Most inquiries receive a reply within one business day. For existing clients with active accounts, Nexus offers priority support through dedicated communication channels. Urgent technical matters are handled by the engineering team to ensure prompt resolution and uninterrupted service.

Does Nexus offer technical support for existing clients?

Yes. Nexus provides continuous technical support to all active clients. This includes assistance with integrations, performance optimization, product updates, and feature deployment. Clients can contact their assigned account manager or the support team directly through the help desk for any operational or technical issue.

Can I reach Nexus through social media or LinkedIn?

Yes. Nexus maintains an active presence on LinkedIn where firms can follow updates, product releases, and industry insights. The page also serves as a direct communication channel for general inquiries, event participation, and partnership discussions.

Can startups contact Nexus or is it only for established prop firms?

Nexus works with both startups and established firms. The platform is modular and can scale according to each company’s size and requirements. Startups benefit from fast deployment, low technical overhead, and the same enterprise-grade tools used by larger operations. This makes Nexus suitable for firms at every stage of growth.
