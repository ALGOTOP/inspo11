Questions? We have Answers

For additional questions, contact our team at hello@nexus.io
What types of partners does Nexus work with?

Nexus works with a broad range of technology and service partners that support the operational, commercial, and regulatory needs of prop firms and brokerages. Examples include marketing agencies, compliance and regulatory specialists, software and infrastructure providers, and other solution partners, but are not limited to these categories.

Do partners need to integrate technically with Nexus?

Not always. Some partnerships involve technical integrations where Nexus connects a partner’s solution into its infrastructure. Other partnerships are non-technical and focus on services or expertise that complement Nexus’s platform without requiring system-level integration.

Can service providers partner without technical integration?

Yes. Nexus works with service providers that add value to clients through advisory, operational, or commercial services without requiring a direct technical connection to the platform.

Is there a certification or approval process for partners?

Yes. All partnerships go through an internal review to ensure strategic fit, reliability, and relevance to Nexus’s clients. This helps maintain a high-quality ecosystem aligned with Nexus’s standards and long-term vision.

Does Nexus offer co-marketing opportunities?

In select cases. Co-marketing or joint visibility may be considered when it aligns with client needs and delivers clear value. Nexus prioritizes meaningful collaboration over broad promotional exposure.

Are partnerships exclusive or non-exclusive?

Most partnerships with Nexus are non-exclusive, allowing flexibility for both parties. In certain cases, exclusivity can be considered if it is strategically aligned and mutually beneficial.
