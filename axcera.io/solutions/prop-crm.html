For additional questions, contact our team at hello@nexus.io
Who is Nexus’s Prop CRM designed for?

Nexus’s Prop CRM is built for serious prop firm operators with a long-term vision. It is designed for firms that require full control over onboarding, challenge logic, risk management, and payouts, and that are focused on building scalable, sustainable businesses across Forex, Futures, or multi-asset models.

How long does it take to launch with Nexus?

Most prop firms can launch within approximately four weeks, provided all required information, integrations, and branding assets are available during onboarding.

Why does Nexus offer a multi-asset prop firm solution?

The future of prop firms is multi-asset. Operating separate firms for Forex and Futures creates duplicated brands, marketing spend, communities, support teams, and systems. Nexus’s multi-asset solution allows firms to run Forex and Futures under a single brand and infrastructure, consolidating operations while keeping trading logic and risk fully separated. This reduces overhead, simplifies management, and creates a more scalable long-term business model.

Is it possible to move an existing prop firm to Nexus?

 Yes. Nexus fully supports migrations from existing prop firm setups. We assist with migrating customer data, account data, and core operational logic, while rebuilding your environment on Nexus’s infrastructure. The migration is handled with no impact on traders, ensuring uninterrupted access and trading activity throughout the process.

How is pricing structured for the Prop CRM?

Nexus operates on a transparent, fixed-fee model. There is no revenue share, no per-trader pricing, and no hidden fees, allowing firms to scale without increasing technology costs.

Can the Trader Area be customized to match our brand?

Yes. The Trader Area is fully yours. It is built to your brand guidelines and business needs, with extensive customization available across layout, flows, and functionality, and custom development supported where required.
