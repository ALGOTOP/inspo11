The Road We’ve Travelled

Key milestones shaping our journey to transform proprietary trading.
2025

November

Operational Scale and Industry Recognition

By late 2025, Nexus’s technology supported over 50 partners globally and more than 1 million unique traders, with over 100,000 trading accounts created each month. This phase marked a shift from rapid onboarding to operational scale and platform consolidation, reinforced by industry recognition as Best Prop Trading Technology Provider.

2025

January

First Public Showcase of the Platform

Nexus presented its technology publicly for the first time at iFX EXPO Dubai. Until this point, growth had been driven entirely by inbound interest, with partners engaging Nexus directly based on the platform’s capabilities and reputation.

2024

September

Expanding Capabilities and Strengthening the Organization

Nexus introduced multi-asset infrastructure with support for Futures, Crypto, and Equities. During this period, the company focused on expanding its team and strengthening internal processes to support continued product development and long-term scalability.

2024

March

Platform Expansion and Early Adoption

Nexus significantly expanded the number of supported trading platforms and integrations. During this period, the platform saw growing adoption from active partners operating across different markets, validating its scalability and operational depth.

2023

July

Entering the Market

Following the completion of the initial platform architecture, Nexus went to market. The first partner signed shortly thereafter, validating the platform’s operating model and confirming demand for a more flexible and automated trading infrastructure.

2022

June

Laying the Technical Foundation

The first line of code was written, marking the beginning of building Nexus’s proprietary trading infrastructure from the ground up. The initial focus was on flexibility, automation, and creating a scalable architecture designed for long-term use.

Our Mission: Help Trading Firms Scale Smarter

Nexus was founded to bring structure, automation, and operational control to proprietary trading firms operating in complex environments. As the platform matured, its infrastructure proved equally well suited to brokerages facing the same challenges around scale, risk, and operational efficiency.

Our vision is to become the underlying infrastructure layer for modern trading firms. We aim to enable operators to run scalable, resilient businesses on technology that adapts as markets, regulations, and business models evolve.
