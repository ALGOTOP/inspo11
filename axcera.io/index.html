Questions? We have Answers

For additional questions, contact our team at hello@nexus.io
Can Nexus support multi-asset prop firms?

Yes. Nexus supports multi-asset prop firms by enabling Forex, Futures, Crypto, and Equities to be managed within a single infrastructure. This allows operators to centralize monitoring, reporting, and risk oversight while applying consistent rules across all asset classes.

What makes Nexus different from other prop firm software or CRMs?

Nexus stands out by offering true infrastructure rather than a single-purpose CRM. It centralizes operations, risk management, and performance tracking while maintaining a transparent licensing model with no revenue share. This gives firms complete control over their data, operations, and profitability.

Does Nexus charge revenue share or hidden fees?

No. Nexus operates on a transparent licensing model with no revenue share, commissions, or hidden costs. Firms maintain full ownership of their revenue streams and benefit from predictable, fixed platform costs.

What kind of support does Nexus provide?

Nexus provides structured onboarding, integration assistance, and continuous technical support, supported by comprehensive documentation. Beyond technical support, Nexus works closely with licensees to review operational and performance data, providing guidance aimed at improving efficiency, risk management, and long-term sustainability.

What trading platforms do you support?

Nexus integrates with all major trading platforms. A few popular ones include MetaTrader 4 and 5, DXtrade, cTrader, Match-Trader, NinjaTrader, Tradovate, and Quantower, among others. This flexibility ensures firms can connect their preferred systems without technical limitations. check all our integrations here

Can we customize the platform to match our brand and specific requirements?

Yes. Nexus builds the Trader Area to your brand guidelines and supports extensive configuration across automation and reporting. Where needed, custom development can be delivered on top of the core infrastructure.

Nexus is a financial technology company delivering core infrastructure and technology solutions for trading firms. With operations and registrations in the United Arab Emirates and Cyprus, Nexus supports clients globally with scalable, data-driven systems.

Nexus does not provide financial services.
